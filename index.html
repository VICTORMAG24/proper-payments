who we are

Welcome to Proper Payments, your one-stop solution for comprehensive merchant services tailored to meet the unique needs of your business! Whether you're a small startup or an established enterprise, we are here to streamline your payment processing, enhance customer experience, and boost your bottom line. With our cutting-edge technology, reliable infrastructure, and dedicated support, we empower businesses to thrive in today's competitive marketplace!

our services

payment processing

We offer secure and efficient payment processing solutions that enable businesses to accept various payment methods, including credit cards, debit cards, mobile wallets, and online payments.

Mobile Payment Solutions

Our mobile card readers, virtual terminals, and mobile apps empower you to process payments securely anytime, anywhere.

point-of-sale (POS) systems

Our user-friendly terminals and software solutions make it easy to process payments, manage inventory, track sales, and generate insightful reports.

subscription billing

Simplify your recurring billing processes with our subscription management services.

E-commerce Solutions

We provide secure payment gateways, shopping cart integration, and customizable online stores that enable you to reach customers globally, 24/7.

Analytics and Reporting

Access comprehensive data on sales, customer behavior, and trends to identify opportunities for growth.
